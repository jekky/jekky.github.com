iGet Download Scheduler
 for iPhone and iPod Touch

iGet is a simple download manager with the distinguish feature about download schdulering.

You can schedule the download of any file in a specific period of time daily, which is very useful e.g. for utilizing your offpeak data of your home broadband in Australia.

It is the only app in the App Store to provide the function of downloading with the schedule option. For every download task, a time period can be specified to allow downloading in that period every day. Manually starting/stoping/resuming a task is also available to users.

The current version 1.0 is just the first version of this app. Any feedback from the users will be appreciated and I will keep update the app to fit your needs!

$0.99

Features

Download Resumption
When the source site supports download resumption, the downloading can be stopped and resumed later.
Schedule Reminder
Push notification will remind you two minutes before the start time of any scheduled task, if you are not in the app.
Midnight Reminder
From 9pm, push notification will remind you to open to app for midnight downloading before you go to sleep.
Download when Lock
You can lock your device and dim the screen, when you are downloading something and it will continue to download.
Open in ... Function
The downloaded files can be opened in other apps supporting them of the device e.g. epub to be open in iBook.
HTTP Sharing
One click for httping sharing of the downloaded files. You can copy/delete files using web browser on your desktop.
